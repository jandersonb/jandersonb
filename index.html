Background

Education

I attended the University of Auckland from 2016--2023, receiving Bachelor's degrees in Science and Arts, Honours in Physics, and a Master's degree in Physics.
I am currently working towards my PhD at Swinburne University of Technology in Melbourne, expecting to finish in 2027. My thesis topic is "A New Census of the Transient Populations of Star Clusters".

Teaching

Between 2018--2023 I was a teaching assistant for various undergraduate maths and physics courses at the University of Auckland. I also participated in the Tuākana programme, which provides tutoring and mentorship for indigenous students at the University.

Outreach

I have volunteered for several outreach events such as the Maths Craft Festival and Mount John Observatory open day, as well as serving on the University of Auckland's Physics Association committee.
I am currently serving on the ANITA steering committee as the student representative.
